This webpage will cover the nessesary ingredients and steps to make Feta Cheese Pasta

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Feta Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • garlic
  • basil

Steps

  1. Use a baking dish, and add tomatoes, olive oil, salt and pepper
  2. Add the block of feta cheese to the center of the baking dish, and either flip it around a couple times to coat it with the olive oil and seasoning, or just add a touch more olive oil and a couple cracks of fresh black pepper.
  3. As soon as it comes out of the oven, add some fresh minced garlic and fresh basil leaves.)
  4. Mix it around immediately so that the garlic softens from the heat in the dish.
  5. Add cooked pasta on top and give everything a stir. The feta and tomatoes will essentially create a sauce for the pasta
  6. Enjoy! :)
